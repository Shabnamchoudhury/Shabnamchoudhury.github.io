My name is Shabnam Choudhury, and I am highly passionate about computer vision and deep learning, specifically in the field of remote sensing. I am now enrolled in a doctoral program at [IIT Bombay](https://www.iitb.ac.in/), under the supervision of [Dr. Biplab Banerjee](https://biplab-banerjee.github.io/). 

I have served as a Research Fellow with the Defence Research and Development Organisation [DRDO](https://drdo.gov.in/drdo/) in India, specifically at [IIT Bhubaneswar](https://www.iitbbs.ac.in/). I have been awarded the esteemed [Prime Minister Research Fellowship](https://www.pmrf.in/) for my Ph.D.
